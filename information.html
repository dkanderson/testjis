Jamaica is the 3rd largest Island in the Caribbean, home to some of the world’s fastest sprinters ever and it’s own genre of music, reggae, made world famous by the legendary singer Bob Marley.

How to get a drivers license in Jamaica

In Jamaica, anyone over the age of 17 may apply for one. It is mandatory for all applicants to complete a writen and driving test. A mandatory fee is also required. There are three categories of licences available in Jamaica General, Private and Motorcycle.
